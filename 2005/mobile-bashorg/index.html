Mobile Bash.org

May 29th, 2005

Now that I realized how useful it is to code pages for cell phone use, another idea popped into my head. I constantly visit AvantSlash on my cell phone because http://slashdot.org/palm sucks. So why not make another geek favorite site cell-phone-friendly too? I bring you misc/bash.php, something that took a whole 5 minutes to code thanks to the great work already done with the Bash.org RSS feed.

Now enjoy the stupidity of IRC chatting at your fingertips wherever you go. So be sure to bookmark it on your phone/PDA for whenever you get bored.
